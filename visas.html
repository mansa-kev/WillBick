Comprehensive Visa Assistance

We support 25 distinct visa categories organized across academic, career, leisure, and immigration pathways. Select a category below to find information and start your application.

1. Tourist Visa

Designed for holidays, sightseeing, visiting family, or leisure travel. Perfect for individuals seeking short-term visits to top destinations worldwide.

Average Processing: 5 - 15 Working Days
Duration: Up to 90 Days
Required: Proof of Funds, Travel Itinerary, Invitation (optional)
Consult on WhatsApp
5. Visitor Visa

For visitors traveling to see close relatives, attend social events, or make short visits where an invitation letter from a host resident or citizen is provided.

Average Processing: 7 - 21 Days
Duration: 3 to 6 Months
Required: Host Details, Relationship Proof, Accommodation Details
Consult on WhatsApp
6. Transit Visa

Required for travelers passing through a country's territory or airport on their way to a third country where layovers exceed airport transit rules.

Average Processing: 2 - 5 Days
Duration: 24 to 96 Hours
Required: Confirmed Ticket to Destination, Valid Visa for Final Country
Consult on WhatsApp
21. Working Holiday Visa

A specialized visa for young adults (usually ages 18-30/35) to holiday in partner nations and work temporary jobs to supplement their travel funds.

Average Processing: 4 - 8 Weeks
Duration: 1 to 2 Years
Required: Age verification, Initial Savings, Health Insurance
Consult on WhatsApp
2. Business Visa

For attending business conferences, negotiations, seminars, client meetings, or exploring investments without entering the local labor market.

Average Processing: 5 - 10 Days
Duration: 30 to 90 Days
Required: Company Dispatch Letter, Invitation from Host Entity
Consult on WhatsApp
3. Work / Employment Visa

Authorizes foreigners to seek and undertake legal employment under a registered employer in the destination country. Typically requires job offer approval.

Average Processing: 4 - 12 Weeks
Duration: Contract Based (1-3 Years Renewable)
Required: Employment Contract, Labor Approval Certificate
Consult on WhatsApp
18. Investor Visa

For high-net-worth individuals making significant capital contributions to businesses or purchasing government bonds in the destination country.

Average Processing: 2 - 6 Months
Duration: Temporary leading to Permanent Residence
Required: Source of Funds Proof, Investment Plan, Capital Threshold Meet
Consult on WhatsApp
19. Entrepreneur & Startup Visa

Specifically structured for innovative founders aiming to establish a scaleable business that generates local employment. Requires an approved business plan.

Average Processing: 6 - 12 Weeks
Duration: 1 to 3 Years
Required: Endorsement letter, Innovative Business Model, Viable funding
Consult on WhatsApp
22. Digital Nomad Visa

Allows remote workers, freelancers, and independent contractors to live in a foreign country while continuing to work for clients based abroad.

Average Processing: 2 - 6 Weeks
Duration: 1 Year (Renewable)
Required: Remote Income Proof, Contract Agreements, Health Coverage
Consult on WhatsApp
24. Crew Member Visa

Designed for transit personnel, airline pilots, flight attendants, and maritime crew members working aboard international vessels or aircraft.

Average Processing: 5 - 10 Days
Duration: Transit limits (up to 29 days per entry)
Required: Crew Employment Contract, Letter of Invitation from Transit Vessel
Consult on WhatsApp
25. Journalist & Media Visa

Required for representatives of foreign media houses, journalists, reporters, camera crew, and editors traveling for work, reporting, or documentary filming.

Average Processing: 10 - 20 Days
Duration: Assignment based (typically 30-90 days)
Required: Press Credentials, Assignment Commission Letter, Media Accreditation
Consult on WhatsApp
4. Student Visa

Enables international students to reside in the destination country to study a registered full-time academic course at an approved school or university.

Average Processing: 4 - 10 Weeks
Duration: Duration of Study Program
Required: Admission Offer (I-20, CAS, COE), Sponsor Funds, Language test
Consult on WhatsApp
16. Religious & Missionary Visa

For religious workers, ministers, missionaries, and members of recognized denominations visiting to perform spiritual work or volunteer work in a local church community.

Average Processing: 4 - 8 Weeks
Duration: 1 to 2 Years
Required: Sponsorship by Religious Entity, Proof of Credentials
Consult on WhatsApp
17. Volunteer Visa

For individuals participating in non-profit, humanitarian, or charitable service programs with established NGOs without receive standard monetary payment.

Average Processing: 2 - 4 Weeks
Duration: 3 to 12 Months
Required: NGO Invitation, Hosting Organization Registration, Self-Funding Proof
Consult on WhatsApp
23. Exchange Visitor Visa

For participants in approved academic, cultural, or work-study exchange programs (e.g. US J-1 visas for research scholars, au pairs, interns).

Average Processing: 3 - 6 Weeks
Duration: Program Specific (usually 3 - 18 months)
Required: Certificate of Eligibility (DS-2019), Program Sponsorship
Consult on WhatsApp
7. Medical Treatment Visa

For individuals seeking specialized healthcare, surgical operations, or medical consultations abroad that are unavailable locally.

Average Processing: 3 - 7 Days
Duration: Treatment duration
Required: Doctor Referrals, Receiving Hospital Confirmation, Medical Fund Proof
Consult on WhatsApp
8. Dependent & Family Visa

Enables immediate family members (spouse, children under age limit) of a valid work or student visa holder to reside in the destination country.

Average Processing: 4 - 12 Weeks
Duration: Co-terminus with Principal Holder
Required: Marriage/Birth Certificates, Sponsor Support Proof
Consult on WhatsApp
9. Spouse & Marriage Visa

Specifically designed for individuals legally married to a citizen or permanent resident of the destination country, allowing them to immigrate.

Average Processing: 3 - 12 Months
Duration: Temporary leading to Permanent Residency
Required: Legal Marriage Certificate, Cohabitation Evidence, Sponsor Financials
Consult on WhatsApp
10. Fiancé(e) Visa

Enables a foreign fiancé(e) to travel to the citizen sponsor's country to get married within a strict timeframe (typically 90 days) and apply for status adjustment.

Average Processing: 5 - 9 Months
Duration: 90 Days (to perform marriage)
Required: Proof of Intent to Marry, Relationship History, Meeting Verification
Consult on WhatsApp
11. Immigrant Visa

A broad category for applicants seeking permanent relocation based on professional skills, green card lotteries, or certified family sponsorships.

Average Processing: 6 - 24 Months
Duration: Indefinite
Required: I-140/I-130 approval, Medical Exams, Police Clearances
Consult on WhatsApp
12. Permanent Residence Visa

Grants a foreign citizen the legal right to reside, work, and study indefinitely in the host country, complete with social welfare rights (e.g. PR card).

Average Processing: 6 - 18 Months
Duration: Indefinite (Card renewal every 5 years)
Required: Residency Points Test, Language Test, Skills Assessment
Consult on WhatsApp
13. Refugee / Asylum Status

For individuals displaced by war, natural disasters, or fleeing targeted persecution due to race, religion, nationality, or political views.

Average Processing: Case by Case basis
Duration: Indefinite / Protected Status
Required: Persecution Evidence, Refugee Agency Referral, UNHCR Assessment
Consult on WhatsApp
14. Diplomatic Visa

Issued to foreign diplomats, government officials, or representatives traveling on official state affairs representing their home nation.

Average Processing: 3 - 7 Days
Duration: Accreditation Period
Required: Diplomatic Passport, Note Verbale from Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Consult on WhatsApp
15. Official Visa

Issued to foreign government employees, military personnel, or international organization representatives visiting on official business.

Average Processing: 3 - 7 Days
Duration: Duty duration
Required: Official passport, Letter of Assignment, Supporting Government Letter
Consult on WhatsApp
20. Retirement Visa

For retirees seeking to spend their retirement years in a foreign country, showing sufficient passive income (pension or investment returns) to support themselves.

Average Processing: 4 - 8 Weeks
Duration: 1 to 5 Years (Renewable)
Required: Pension Verification, Health Check, Passive Income Statements
Consult on WhatsApp
