International Scholarships

We bridge the gap between talented students and international institutions, helping you secure admissions, sponsorships, and visa approvals.

100% Fully Funded Admission Pathways

Fully funded tracks cover 100% of university tuition fees, provide a monthly living allowance (stipend), cover overseas student health cover (OSHC), and often include round-trip economy flights. These are highly competitive and require stellar academic records.

Typical Requirements:

  • First-Class Honours or Strong Upper Second Class (GPA 3.5+ equivalent)
  • English Proficiency test scores (IELTS 7.0+ or TOEFL 95+)
  • 2-3 Strong Academic and Professional Recommendation Letters
  • Compelling Statement of Purpose (SOP) and Research Proposal (for PhD/Master's)
Discuss Eligibility on WhatsApp

Partial Scholarships & Tuition waivers

Partial scholarships cover a percentage of tuition fees (ranging from 15% to 50% reduction). These are merit-based, sport-based, or region-based, and are widely accessible for average-to-high scoring students looking to decrease the overall cost of international study.

Typical Requirements:

  • Good academic score (GPA 2.8+ equivalent or B plain average)
  • Language proof (IELTS 6.0/6.5 or Duolingo/Medium of Instruction waivers)
  • Proof of balance tuition funds and living cost support
Discuss Eligibility on WhatsApp

Self-Sponsored Academic Placements

Self-sponsored applications are perfect for students with financial capability who want fast admissions to top global universities. We coordinate direct applications, advise on required banking statement formats, review documentation, and handle the student visa files to guarantee approvals.

Typical Requirements:

  • Passing grades in high school or undergraduate certificate
  • Proof of funds / Bank statements (usually 28-day holding rules apply)
  • Sponsor commitment letter (parents or guardians)
