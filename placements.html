Global Job Placements

Connect with overseas recruiters and complete legal work permit processing for professional, technical, and skilled roles abroad.

Where We Place Candidates

We work in cooperation with certified international recruiters to assist qualified Kenyan professionals in securing employment contracts and processing legal work permits.

Europe (Poland, Germany, UK)

Pathways for skilled software developers, mechanical technicians, logistics experts, and certified healthcare staff (nurses and carers).

North America (Canada & USA)

Facilitation for provincial nomination work programs, intracompany transfers, and corporate sponsorships for highly specialized professionals.

Middle East & Gulf Region

Reliable service-industry, construction supervision, hospitality manager, and security professional placements with vetted, safe employers.

In-Demand Industries

Candidates with qualifications or experience in these sectors have a significantly higher chance of quick placement and work permit approval:

Industry Roles Placed Key Locations
Healthcare Registered Nurses, Caregivers UK, Germany, Ireland
Tech & Engineering Software Engineers, Technicians Germany, Poland, Canada

Hospitality Hotel Managers, F&B Staff, Chefs UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia

Eligibility Criteria

Before beginning a placement file, ensure you meet these standard international recruitment thresholds:

  • Minimum 2-3 years active experience in your target sector.
  • Relevant academic diplomas, degrees, or vocational trade certificates.
  • Clean criminal record and certificate of good conduct (DCI Kenya).
  • Valid Kenyan passport (minimum 18 months validity remaining).
  • Basic English proficiency (or language certificates for Germany/Poland).

Application & Placement Timeline

What to expect when processing an international placement file with Willbick:

1

Profile Verification & Document Audit

We review your CV, academic papers, and job experience to match your skills with active international recruitment demands.

2

Employer Interview Coordination

Qualified candidates are prepped and presented to international hiring partners for virtual interviews and technical evaluations.

3

Offer Letter & Work Permit Filing

Once selected, we facilitate contract signatures and initiate the legal work permit application with foreign labor departments.

4

Embassy Visa Appointment & Flight Booking

We prepare your embassy file, schedule your interview, coordinate medical checks, and secure your travel booking upon visa issuance.
